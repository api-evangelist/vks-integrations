Smart manufacturing extends beyond automation alone. The article explores five practical implementations - digital work instructions, connected workers, smart tool integrations, real-time visibility, and data-driven continuous improvement - that help manufacturers standardize processes, enhance quality, minimize downtime, and create collaborative operations where technology and people work together.
